Released Games

Bloodbound Alchemy

In a world that fears mysterious magic, you are a sorcerer living a double life. To the public, you are a humble sorcerer providing essential potions to the local village. In the shadows you practice the forbidden art of Black Magic, with blood staining your hands.

The secret to your highly effective potions? The villagers themselves. You must strategically "harvest" your neighbors to fuel your craft, using human remains as your primary reagents. Be careful, don't get caught.

Among The Innocent

You are a member of an exorcism cult which dedicated to sweep out the world of the vampire. However, you have a dark secret: you are a vampire yourself. To survive, you must balance up tasks and member ranks within the cult, participating in hunts while hiding your true identity from your "family" and suppress your thirst for blood.
